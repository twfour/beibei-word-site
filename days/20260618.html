01 CONTEXT / 背景介绍

读前先知道

伊朗队赴美国参加世界杯时，体育赛事正与紧张的美伊关系、战争阴影和旅行限制重叠。文章所述背景中，部分伊朗代表团成员未能取得美国签证，球队也因安全考虑把训练基地从亚利桑那州改到墨西哥蒂华纳。因此，伊朗队每次赴洛杉矶比赛都要面对额外的飞行、入境检查和恢复时间压力；对球员和在美伊朗社群而言，比赛也承载了远超比分的政治与身份意义。

02 ROADMAP / 内容简介

文章会讲什么

文章以伊朗队 2 比 2 战平新西兰后的采访开场。前锋迈赫迪·塔雷米没有回避媒体，而是公开抱怨签证、通勤、安全和缺乏支持等现实困难。报道随后把视线从球场扩展到洛杉矶的伊朗侨民：有人支持国家队，有人把球队视为伊朗政权的象征，也有人试图把足球与政治分开。文章通过球员、官员、球迷和抗议者的不同声音说明，这场世界杯之旅既是体育竞赛，也是战争、移民身份、国家认同与外交关系交织的公共事件。

03 EASIER ENGLISH / 简明改写

Read the story in easier English

Iran began its World Cup in the United States with a 2–2 draw against New Zealand. After the game, Iranian striker Mehdi Taremi wanted to talk about more than football. He said the situation was a disaster for his team. The United States had strict travel rules for Iranians, and eleven members of Iran’s football group could not get visas. The team first planned to train in Arizona, but it moved its base to Tijuana in Mexico because of safety worries. Before the match, the players flew to Los Angeles. The journey and immigration checks took a long time. They had to return to Mexico soon after the game, so they had less time to rest. The match was also emotional for Iranian people living in Los Angeles. Many came to support the team, while some protesters said the team represented the Iranian government. Other fans wanted to keep football separate from politics. For Iran, this World Cup is not only about winning matches. The players must deal with travel problems, political tension and pressure from different groups. Their experience shows how war and international relations can affect sport.

长难句分析

从主干到修饰层级，拆开再读。

SENTENCE 1
Iranian striker Mehdi Taremi had plenty to say on Monday after his team drew with New Zealand, 2-2, in a wildly entertaining, creative, and tense World Cup game filled with passion and meaning stretching far beyond the pitch.

1. 主句（核心主干） Iranian striker Mehdi Taremi had plenty to say on Monday 主语：Iranian striker Mehdi Taremi Iranian：形容词，定语，修饰 striker striker：名词（前锋），中心名词 Mehdi Taremi：同位语，说明前锋是谁 谓语：had（have 的过去式，实义动词） 宾语：plenty 后置定语：to say（不定式作后置定语，修饰 plenty，表 “想说的话”） 时间状语：on Monday

主句翻译 伊朗前锋迈赫迪・塔雷米在周一有满腹话想说。

2. 时间状语从句（after 引导） after his team drew with New Zealand, 2-2 引导词：after（连词，表 “在…… 之后”） 从句主干： 主语：his team 谓语：drew（draw 过去式，体育语境：战平） 状语：with New Zealand（介词短语，对手） 插入语 2-2：同位语，补充平局比分

从句含义：在他的球队 2 比 2 战平新西兰之后

3. 地点介词短语（in … game，修饰从句动作 drew） in a wildly entertaining, creative, and tense World Cup game in + 宾语 game，作从句的地点 / 场景状语 a wildly entertaining, creative, and tense：三个并列形容词前置修饰 game wildly 副词修饰 entertaining

4/ 两层后置定语，层层修饰 game 第一层：filled with passion and meaning 过去分词短语作后置定语，修饰 game 还原定语从句：which was filled with passion and meaning 含义：饱含热忱与深意的（比赛） 第二层：stretching far beyond the pitch 现在分词短语后置，修饰前面的 passion and meaning 还原定语从句：(passion and meaning) which stretch far beyond the pitch stretch far beyond：远远超越 the pitch：球场（绿茵场）

SENTENCE 2
He was more venting about the practical challenges of playing World Cup games in a country, the United States, that started, along with Israel, a war against Iran and has instituted a strict travel ban against Iranians.

1. 主句主干 He was more venting about the practical challenges 主语：He 谓语：was venting was：助动词 venting：vent 的现在分词，共同构成过去进行时 more：副词，修饰 venting，表 “更多地、主要是” 介词宾语（about 的宾语）：the practical challenges practical：形容词作前置定语，修饰 challenges 后置定语（of 所有格）：of playing World Cup games 动名词短语修饰 challenges：“征战世界杯带来的现实阻碍”

2. 多层介词定语，层层嵌套修饰 challenges of playing World Cup games in a country in a country：介词短语作后置定语，修饰 playing…，说明赛事举办地

3. 同位语插入语 , the United States,

两个逗号之间是 a country 的同位语，解释这个国家就是美国，可直接删去不破坏句子主干。

4. 定语从句（that 引导，修饰先行词 the United States） that started, along with Israel, a war against Iran and has instituted a strict travel ban against Iranians. 先行词：the United States（单数国家，从句谓语用三单,along with 是介词短语） 从句拆分并列双谓语 从句主语：that（指代美国） 并列谓语 1：started a war against Iran 插入分隔状语：along with Israel（随同以色列，不改变主语单复数） 宾语：a war against Iran 并列谓语 2：has instituted a strict travel ban against Iranians 现在完成时 has instituted，与过去式 started 并列，描述美国先后 / 持续采取的两项行动 institute：及物动词，出台、颁布（禁令、政策） travel ban：旅行禁令
