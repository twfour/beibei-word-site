长难句分析

从主干到修饰层级，拆开再读。

SENTENCE 1
They put any drowsiness down to bad speakers rambling, attribute a reduction in public appearances to more time spent in the situation room due to the Iran war, and say he regularly works 12-hour days, if not longer, when your average 80-year-old might do the crossword and then have a nap. 第一部分：They put any drowsiness down to bad speakers rambling 核心短语：put A down to B 固定搭配 = 把 A 归咎于 B；认为 A 是由 B 造成 A：any drowsiness （偶尔的困倦、昏沉） B：bad speakers rambling 语法点：rambling 现在分词作后置定语，修饰前面的 bad speakers 还原：bad speakers who are rambling ramble /ˈræmbl/v. 东拉西扯、讲话拖沓、言之无物 译：讲话絮絮叨叨的发言者 句式拆解：主语 + 谓语短语 + 宾语 + 原因状语 第二部分：attribute a reduction in public appearances to more time spent in the situation room due to the Iran war 核心短语：attribute A to B 固定搭配 = 将 A 归因于 B，和上一组 put...down to 同义，作者换词避免重复。 A：a reduction in public appearances 公开露面次数减少 B：more time spent in the situation room 花在战情室的时间变多 语法点 1：spent in the situation room 过去分词短语作后置定语，修饰 more time 还原：more time that is spent in the situation room （时间是 “被花费”，故用过去分词表被动） 语法点 2：due to the Iran war 介词短语作原因状语，修饰前面整段内容，说明 “为何要待在战情室”。 句式拆解：并列谓语 + 宾语 + 定语 + 后置原因状语 第三部分：and say he regularly works 12-hour days, if not longer, when your average 80-year-old might do the crossword and then have a nap. 主句：and say + 宾语从句，省略引导词 that（口语 / 新闻文体中常省） 宾语从句主干：he regularly works 12-hour days 他每日正常工作 12 小时。 插入语：if not longer 完整形式：if he does not work longer than 12 hours （有 12 小时）甚至更久。 时间状语从句：when your average 80-year-old might do the crossword and then have a nap 由从属连词 when 引导，表对比（而非单纯 “当…… 时”），译为而。 your average 80-year-old：普通八旬老人（固定表达，your 此处表泛指人群） 从句内部并列谓语：do the crossword（玩填字游戏） + have a nap（小憩）
SENTENCE 2
Many who have known the president for a long time (his age means he has been around for nearly a third of America’s existence, with Harry S Truman president when he was born) say what is most notable is how he hasn’t really changed at all, from wisecracks to a taste for vengeance and an appetite for reading all clippings about him, rewarding or lashing out accordingly. -

1. 主语部分 Many who have known the president for a long time Many：主句主语，泛指 “许多人” who have known the president for a long time：限制性定语从句，修饰先行词 Many 关系代词 who 在从句中作主语；从句时态为现在完成时，强调动作从过去持续到现在。 2. 插入语（补充信息，语法上独立，不影响主句结构） (his age means he has been around for nearly a third of America’s existence, with Harry S Truman president when he was born) 整段是括号插入语，分为两部分： ① 分句 1：his age means he has been around for nearly a third of America’s existence 主干：his age means + 宾语从句 宾语从句：he has been around for nearly a third of America’s existence has been around：现在完成时，表 “在世、历经岁月”；for 引导时间状语。 ② 分句 2：with Harry S Truman president when he was born with 复合结构（独立主格），作伴随状语，补充出生时的时代背景 结构：with + 名词(Harry S Truman) + 名词(president)（名词作宾语补足语，表身份） 内部嵌套 when 引导的时间状语从句：when he was born 语法说明：with + n. + n. 是高频用法，表 “某人身兼某身份”，本句即 “他出生时，杜鲁门正值总统任期”。

3. 主句谓语 say：主句谓语动词（一般现在时）

4. 宾语从句（say 后面全部内容，省略引导词 that） 完整从句：what is most notable is how he hasn’t really changed at all, from wisecracks to a taste for vengeance and an appetite for reading all clippings about him, rewarding or lashing out accordingly. 这一整段是双层名词性从句嵌套： 第一层：宾语从句内部的主语 what is most notable what 引导主语从句，在从句中同时作主语（= the thing that is most notable）。 第二层：主语从句后的系表结构 is how he hasn’t really changed at all is：系动词 how 引导表语从句，作表语； 从句时态：现在完成时 hasn’t changed，强调 “至今未曾改变”。

5. 介词短语作范围状语 from wisecracks to a taste for vengeance and an appetite for reading all clippings about him from ... to ... 固定搭配，表从…… 到……，用来列举 “他未曾改变” 的具体方方面面，修饰前面 hasn’t really changed。 拆解短语： wisecracks 俏皮话 a taste for vengeance 报复心

an appetite for reading all clippings about him 热衷于翻看所有关于自己的剪报

6. 句末现在分词短语（伴随状语） rewarding or lashing out accordingly 语法：现在分词短语作伴随状语，逻辑主语为句子主语 he（特朗普）； 并列结构：rewarding 和 lashing out 两个现在分词由 or 连接； accordingly 副词作方式状语，表 “相应地、据此”； 含义：（他）会根据报道好坏，或是奖赏他人，或是出言抨击。
