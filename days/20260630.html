01 CONTEXT / 背景介绍

读前先知道

这篇文章把中美人工智能竞争放在日常生活、国家战略和地缘政治三个层面来观察。

美国舆论常把 AI 竞赛理解为谁先创造出超级智能，关注芯片、算力、人才和尖端模型。

文章中的中国视角则更强调“人工智能作为基础设施”：它不是一个遥远的神话目标，而是被嵌入外卖、交通、教育、医疗、城市治理和供应链中的实用系统。

02 ROADMAP / 内容简介

文章会讲什么

文章先用作者在中国生活中的 AI 场景开篇：幼儿园照片识别、外卖系统、刷脸进站和无人驾驶出租车。

随后，作者对比中美两种 AI 路线：美国追求最强大的超级智能，中国则在严格监管下推动 A.I.+，把 AI 扩散到公共服务和产业体系。

后半部分进一步说明，中国希望用 AI 改善农村教育、医疗、养老、极端天气应对和绿色能源转型，同时把整套 AI 管理方案输出到全球市场。

文章的核心判断是：美国也许会率先发射“超级智能飞船”，但中国可能先把 AI 变成地球上可运行的基础设施。

03 EASIER ENGLISH / 简明改写

The article compares the way China and the United States think about artificial intelligence. In the United States, many leaders believe the country must win the AI race against China.

They often focus on chips, talent, power grids and the dream of building superintelligence. The writer says China is moving in a different direction.

In China, AI is already part of daily life. It helps identify children in school photos, supports food delivery, and appears in trains, taxis and city systems.

This can feel uncomfortable because it includes surveillance, but it is also convenient. China’s strategy is called A.I.+.

It treats AI like infrastructure. The government wants cheap and useful AI tools to spread through public services.

AI may help rural students learn, help doctors diagnose diseases, support elderly care, predict extreme weather and improve green energy systems. The article also says China can export this approach.

Instead of selling only one product, China may sell whole systems: energy, transport, telecoms, surveillance and AI management. Other countries may buy these solutions because they are practical and good enough.

The main message is that America may still build the most powerful AI first. But China may use AI more deeply in ordinary life, hospitals, schools and roads.

This difference matters for the future of global power.

长难句分析

从主干到修饰层级，拆开再读。

SENTENCE 1
In the quest for superintelligence, the U.S. government is encouraging private firms to move full speed ahead, regulation be damned.

1. In the quest for superintelligence 【介词短语作句首状语，表目的 / 背景】 In：介词 the quest：名词，中心词 for superintelligence：介词后置定语，修饰 quest quest for sth = 对…… 的追逐、求索 功能：交代主句动作发生的大背景 —— 在追逐超级智能的大趋势下

2. 主句：the U.S. government is encouraging private firms to move full speed ahead 主干句型：S + encourage + 宾语 + to do（动词不定式作宾语补足语） 主语：the U.S. government 谓语：is encouraging 时态：现在进行时，表当下持续推行的政策 动词搭配：encourage sb. to do sth. 鼓励某人做某事 宾语：private firms 宾语补足语：to move full speed ahead（不定式短语） move：不定式核心动词 full speed ahead：固定副词短语，作方式状语修饰 move 本义航海用语：全速前进，此处指毫无约束地加速研发

3. 重点难点：regulation be damned 【独立虚拟结构，古体 be 型虚拟语气】 （1）语法本质 这是英语传统书面语，独立分句，无连词，自带主谓，作评注性状语，用来表达说话人轻蔑、不在乎的态度。 完整省略还原：(Let) regulation be damned. 属于命令式虚拟语气，规则：不论主语单复数，动词一律用原形 be。 （2）成分拆分 regulation：主语（名词，监管法规） be：原形系动词（虚拟专用，不随时态 / 人称变化） damned：过去分词作表语，本义 “被诅咒的”，引申为 “不值一提、随便抛在一边” （3）句式逻辑 逗号隔开，独立于主句之外，不与主句主谓产生语法连接，仅从语义上补充作者批判态度： 政府只管催促企业全速发展，至于监管？全然不顾。 同类同结构例句（be 永远原形）

Consequences be damned. 后果什么的，一概不管。 Rules be damned. 规则统统靠边站。

SENTENCE 2
The Chinese A.I.+ strategy is practical and comprehensible to the local population in a way that the U.S. strategy simply is not, which may explain why the Chinese appear so much more optimistic about A.I. than Americans.

1. 主句 The Chinese A.I.+ strategy is practical and comprehensible to the local population 主语：The Chinese A.I.+ strategy 系动词：is 并列表语：practical and comprehensible 介词短语作状语：to the local population comprehensible to sb = 普通人能看懂、易于理解

2. 方式状语结构 + 限制性定语从句 in a way that the U.S. strategy simply is not in a way 介词短语，作方式状语，修饰前面主句； 含义：在某种层面、以一种…… 的方式。 that 引导定语从句，先行词 a way； 从句主干：the U.S. strategy simply is not 省略了重复的表语 (practical and comprehensible)，完整还原： the U.S. strategy simply is not practical and comprehensible 翻译：美国的战略却完全做不到这一点。

3. 非限制性定语从句（逗号 which） , which may explain … which 关系代词，指代前面一整句话（中国 AI + 方案接地气、民众易懂，美国方案则不然这件事）； 从句谓语：may explain

4. why 引导宾语从句（作 explain 的宾语） why the Chinese appear so much more optimistic about A.I. than Americans 引导词 why 主语：the Chinese 系动词：appear 表语：optimistic 修饰比较级：so much more 比较状语：than Americans 完整比较：the Chinese are more optimistic than Americans (are optimistic)
